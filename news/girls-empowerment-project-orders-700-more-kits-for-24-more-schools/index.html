Girls’ Empowerment Project Orders 700 More Kits for 24 More Schools

8/22/2025

Thanks to the California 49ers Club, President-Elect Jose de Anda and President Andrea Spessard just ordered hundreds more reusable menstrual management kids for more than a score more schools to empower girls to become confident, educated women. These will be distributed beginning in the second week of September when school is in session again.
