THIS WEEK AT ROTARY MIDDAY 

Rotary Club Assembly
​​​​Tuesday, June 2, 2026 @ 9:30am
Sala Quetzal in la Biblioteca or ZOOM
Videos of Past Speakers Click HERE
The first Tuesday of every month is set aside for our Club Assembly.

Club Assemblies are an important opportunity for us to come together as a club to:
  • Hear updates about our current and upcoming projects and initiatives

  • Exchange ideas and strengthen our fellowship as Rotarians

  • Join us, especially if you have thought about becoming a Member.​
Our meetings take place in the Sala Quetzal in the Biblioteca Pública, which opens at 8:15 a.m. via the Relox entrance near Insurgentes. Cafe Zenteno serves breakfast before the meeting for $150 MXN in cash.

If you would prefer to view the meeting via ZOOM, complete the form below and we will send you the link.

Rotary is where neighbors, friends and problem-solvers share ideas, join leaders, and take action to create lasting change. For more information, contact President, Andrea Spessard at aspessard@rotarysmamidday.org or Vice President of Club Services, Robin Loving at robin@rotarysmamidday.org.
